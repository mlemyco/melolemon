MELON

TRAITS: Young, slow, lazy, cute, a bit of a slacker.
DESIRES: To bring color and interesting designs to their own world.
STRENGTHS: Creates very elaborate, beautiful, and cute designs.
WEAKNESSES: Is very slow when creating art, perfectionist.
LIKES: Peace and quiet. When Lemon is being quiet. Relaxing and going slow.
DISLIKES: Rushing, loud noises.
PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES: A small white bunny that walks on it’s legs with a pink dress on.

LEMON

TRAITS: Older of the 2 bunnies, adventurous, cool, an efficient worker.
DESIRES: To bring color and interesting designs to their own world.
STRENGTHS: Is efficient and creates designs and art at a steady pace.
WEAKNESSES: Cannot easily create designs outside of their own comfort zone.
LIKES: Being cool, confidence.
DISLIKES: Being quiet, going slowly, staying in one place.
PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES: A small white bunny that walks on it’s legs with a yellow dress on.
