melolemon Launches Physical Products

melolemon is excited to announce their new line of physical products, giving customers the opportunity to add design to YOUR world.

melolemon is a two-person artist team who bring their wild ideas to life. From whimsical characters to full art pieces, they are able to turn their imagination into a reality. They are thrilled to be able to offer their art as physical products so that customers can appreciate them in a whole new way.

Their products include sticker sheets and pins, with a variety of designs to choose from. Later this month, customers will get to see the products in person at their live event. This provides a unique opportunity to see the products before purchasing them and select the designs that speak to you.

Here’s what melolemon has to offer:

• Sticker Sheets - Featuring designs such as forest animals and abstract art

• Pins - Aspire to your wildest dreams with these inspiring pins

melolemon is a team of two artists that love bringing their wild creations to life. They are passionate about offering their art as physical products, so that customers can appreciate them in all their glory. With their products, you can now add design to YOUR world.
